Research Paper

Download Research Paper (PDF)

My research entailed designing, programming, and building a device to induce exercise in fruit flies with spinal muscular atrophy (SMA). My goal was to test the effects of various intensities of exercise coupled with dietary restriction on the progression of SMA degenerative symptoms. My research found that (1) The Drosophila Rotation Device is a viable device in inducing exercise in fruit flies and (2) The independent benefits of exercise and dietary restriction can be concurrently counterproductive.

Drosophila Rotation Device (DRD)

The DRD was designed with Autodesk Fusion and 3D printed. I used an Arduino Nano, Breadboard, mini DC motor, and C++ to build and program the device. The DRD's clamps holds vials of fruit flies, and while spinning the flies continuously fly up, and fall down, inducing exercise.

Respirometry

The respirometer functions to indicate relative fly metabolic activity post-exercise and diet. Flies placed in pipettes expell CO2, which reacts with NaOH in pipette creating a vacuum and pulling up water. Greater displacement of water indicates higher fly metabolic rate.

My Coding Projects

Edmund

At HPoint, I entirely developed the back-end and UI/UX of a tool to automate proprietary sourcing. Edmund sources company names, locations, websites, google stars/reviews based on a given industry and geography, and saves data in an .xlsx file.
